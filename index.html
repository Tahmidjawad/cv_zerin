About

A quick snapshot that reads fast — not like an essay.

What I do

I work on the full pipeline from device-level understanding to system-level reliability: CMOS layout and verification, radiation effects modeling, and photonic measurements. I enjoy building rigorous workflows—simulation to validation—and turning results into clean, explainable engineering decisions.

  • Analog/mixed-signal layout in deep sub-micron CMOS
  • Radiation environments & SEE analysis (OMERE/FASTRAD/G4SEE)
  • Optical fiber RIA measurement and dose-behavior analysis
What I’m aiming for

I want roles and research directions that connect microelectronics reliability with photonic technology: radiation-hardened design, robust sensing links, and data-driven verification. My strength is combining tools, measurements, and documentation into dependable results.
